About IWOC

Step into the world of open-source development and elevate your skills to new heights with the Innogeeks Winter of Code. This online program is specially crafted to introduce new contributors to the fascinating world of open-source software development. Join us for a month-long event that will immerse you in a collaborative and challenging environment, where you will have the opportunity to contribute to a variety of projects hand-picked by our team. Showcase your talents and be rewarded with exclusive swags and goodies for being one of the top performers. Be a part of our mission to foster an open-source culture at KIET Group of Institutions and embark on a thrilling journey of personal and professional growth.

Frequently asked questions

How many team members can I invite?

It is an individual event.

Do i need to be good in development for taking part?

No! You can be a beginner and still contribute, we're there to help you out.

How to contribute?

The project links are availabe on the site. One can use their github id for making the contributions

Is the event online/offline?

Hybrid. Speaker Sessions will be both online and offline.

Can I participate in IWOC both as a maintainer and a Contributor?

No you can only participate as one.

Are participants from other colleges allowed to participate in IWoC 2023?

If you are not from KIET yet still wish to participate , you can contact us at innogeeks@kiet.edu
